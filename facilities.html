FACILITIES

Country Roads Motor Inn Facilities

We offer a large range of facilities and services, including free Wi-Fi, BBQ facilities and a large plunge pool. Towels for the pool are available at reception.

We have cots for little ones, extra fold-up beds for the not so little. Crockery and cutlery to enjoy your local meal, coffee, tea and biscuits, TV & Microwave.

If you're a business person and wish to stay one or a few nights, use your room for business, let us know. We have a wide range of services to assist you with...including those moments when you've come from Melbourne or Sydney and forgotten something or need some assistance. We have keyboards, mice, external 4K monitors, card readers...and a little IT expertise... Just ask.

Why Choose Us?

Our friendly, hard working staff are fully trained to the highest standards in cleanliness. We use hospital-grade cleaning chemicals to ensure your room is in peak condition.

When you walk into our rooms you will notice the difference. Underlays, sheets and blankets are all laundered prior to your arrival, and your room is prepared individually by staff and checked by management.

That is the Country Roads Narrandera difference. You can be assured the quality of your room is second to none, and if there's anything you need or anything we can do for you, all you have to do is ask.

Great Rates

With a large range of quality rooms and configurations, we can tailor a room for you that meets your budget.

Reception

Reception is open until 9:30 pm. For late arrivals, a keylock system is available but must be arranged beforehand.

Free Wi-Fi

Enjoy free Wi-Fi with local 5G speeds between 400–500Mb/s. Bring your own modem to take full advantage.

Plunge Pool

Cool off in our sparkling clean plunge pool — the perfect way to relax on a hot day.

Complimentary Toiletries

Quality shampoo, conditioner, body wash, hand wash, lotion, and shaving cream in every room.

Off Street Parking

Secure off-street parking available near your room entrance. Height clearance: 2.1m.
