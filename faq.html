Frequently Asked Questions

What time is check-in and check-out?
Check-in starts at 2:00 PM, and check-out is at 11:00 AM.
Is parking available?
Yes, we offer free on-site parking for all our guests.
Do you offer free Wi-Fi?
Yes, complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi is available throughout the motel.
Are pets allowed?
Unfortunately, pets are not allowed on the property.
Is breakfast included?
Yes, a continental breakfast is included with your stay.
What amenities do you offer?
We offer a pool, gym, spa, and a 24-hour front desk service.
Is there a shuttle service?
Yes, we provide complimentary shuttle service to the airport.
Can I cancel my booking?
Yes, cancellations are free up to 24 hours before check-in.
Do you have a fitness center?
Yes, the fitness center is open 24/7 for all guests.
Is smoking allowed?
No, smoking is prohibited in all indoor areas.
