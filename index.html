ABOUT US

At the Country Roads Motor Inn, our aim is to make your stay friendly, comfortable, and relaxed – even if it’s just for a one-night stopover. We are located at the top of town, on the corner of East Street (Main Street) and Whitton Street (Newell Highway), just 100 metres from the town centre. We offer late-night check-in, incredibly comfortable queen-size beds with clean, crisp sheets, and steaming hot showers – everything you need to completely relax. We have a range of room configurations to suit all types of travellers, including business people and families. Sorry – we are not pet-friendly. Check out our Rooms page to choose the perfect room to indulge in during your stay. We offer free Wi-Fi, and local Telstra 5G coverage within the motel boundaries is superfast – reaching speeds of up to (and over) 500 Mb/s. Our plunge pool area also features BBQ facilities, with all utensils available from reception.

Things to Do

LOCAL ATTRACTIONS

At Country Roads Narrandera, we recommend tourist attractions we have personally experienced, so you know you’ll thoroughly enjoy your visit.

Lake Talbot Water Park

Perfect for a hot day! Kids will have an amazing time, and it’s also great for swimming laps. All the fun of a theme park, right here in Narrandera.

Address: 1 Lake Drive, Narrandera
Phone: (02) 6959 1990

Lilley Pilley Winery

Award-winning wines by Rob Fiumara, even served in Qantas first class. Just a short drive from Narrandera — a must for wine lovers.

Hours: 10am–5pm (Mon–Sat), Sunday by appointment
Phone: (02) 6953 4069

Altina Wildlife Park

Unique Zoo-fari tours on horse-drawn or motorised carts. See magnificent animals up close during feeding times — book early!

Address: Sturt Highway, Darlington Point
Phone: 0412 060 342
